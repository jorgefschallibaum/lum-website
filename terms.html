1. Booking & Payment

Bookings at LUM Sunset Villas are made directly with us by message or email. A reservation is confirmed once we have agreed your dates and received any deposit requested. Prices are quoted in Thai Baht (THB) and may vary by season.

2. Cancellation

Our cancellation terms will be confirmed in writing at the time of booking. As a guideline, cancellations made well in advance may receive a partial or full refund of any deposit, while cancellations close to the arrival date may be non-refundable. Please contact us directly to discuss changes to your stay.

3. Check-in & House Guidelines

  • Standard arrival and departure times will be confirmed with your booking; we try to be flexible where possible.
  • The villas are designed for restful stays. Guests are asked to be considerate of others sharing the hillside.
  • Please treat the villa, pool, and surroundings with care.

We use this information solely to respond to you and arrange your booking. We do not sell your information, and we do not share it except as needed to provide your stay (for example, arranging transfers).
